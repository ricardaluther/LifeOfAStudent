# LifeOfAStudent
Welcome to Life of a Student! You are tired of all these assignements? No worries just shoot them with your pencil and many more interesting powerups! 
But be careful with your enemies and dont fall asleep when many different assignment attack you!
Can you reach a score above 200? Or do you get exmatriculated too early?
